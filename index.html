    • The second is by the vending machines.
    • The third is in apartment #4 hiding behind a plant.
    • The fourth is in apartment #4 on the counter.
    • The fifth is on the wall near the door to apartment #7.
    • The sixth is to the left after entering apartment #7 behind the AC unit.
    • The seventh, eighth, ninth, tenth and eleventh extinguishers are in the room with a goon laying in bed.
    • The last two (twelfth and thirteenth) are in the area near the exit staircase.
  • Kick the skeletons.
    • There's a skeleton behind a black door. (walkthrough)
  • Do the challenges for this level.
    • Complete the level.
    • Complete the level without killing any enemies. It's recommended to use firefighters shoes.
    • Kill an enemy by kicking a grenade.

Firepit (Level 4-7)

  • Do the challenges for this level.
    • Complete the level.
    • Complete the level without killing an enemy. Don't worry about enemies killed by other enemies, they won't count against you. When you get to the door with an eye, there is an alternate path to the left you must take. Maze of doors ahead. Follow this sequence for the doors: Forward through first door of maze, right, forward through bathroom, forward through orange room, forward through cactus room, forward through #3 sign room, left through skeleton room, right through money room, forward through chandelier room, right at #7 sign, forward, forward through flies, forward through concrete brick room, forward through purple room, forward through #8 sign room, right through room with grate floor, forward, forward, forward, and exit maze through eye door.
    • Ignite 40 goons. It's recommended to use firefighters or holy sandals. You need to kill nearly every enemy in the level with a flamethrower. (Except the flamethrower enemies themselves as they're immune to fire.) Make sure to activate the switch on this level or you won't find enough enemies.
